To establish whether the client has been offered testing for blood-borne virus (BBV) infection (Hepatitis B, Hepatitis C and HIV).
 
Format: 2 character alpha numeric
 
Value
Meaning
01
The client has already been tested in the last 12 months or are currently being treated for BBV virus infection
02
Testing offered and accepted.
03
Testing offered and refused.
04
Testing not offered.
