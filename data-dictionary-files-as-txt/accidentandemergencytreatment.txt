A broad list of types of treatment or guidance which may be provided to a patient as a result of an Accident and Emergency Attendance.
Notes:
•     The treatment fields do not directly relate to any of the diagnosis fields, but record any treatments that have taken place.
•     Up to 6 treatments can be reported in this section of the Data Set
