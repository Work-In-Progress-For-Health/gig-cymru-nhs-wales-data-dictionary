The main composition of critical care bed types for the ward.
Format: 2 character alpha-numeric
Value
Meaning
02
Level 2 beds only where patients require more detailed observation or intervention including support for a single failing organ system or post-operative care and those 'stepping down' from higher levels of care
03
Level 3 beds only where patients require advanced respiratory support alone or basic respiratory support together with support of at least two organ systems. This level includes beds for all complex patients requiring support for multi-organ failure
05
Flexible critical care beds where there is a mix of level 2 and level 3 beds
90
Temporary use of non critical care bed
