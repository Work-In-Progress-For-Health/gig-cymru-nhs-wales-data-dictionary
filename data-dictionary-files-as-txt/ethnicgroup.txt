This is the ethnic group of the patient, as selected by the patient. The patient is the arbiter of the information. Classifications are based on the ethnic group data categories used in the 2011 Census and the information recorded about ethnic group must be obtained by asking the patient / client.
 
Format: 2 character alpha- numeric – AB, where the first character (A) is a value taken from the list in the table below, and the second character (B) is a locally defined value to be used in conjunction with the nationally defined values. If no further local breakdown is required, the second character (B) should be filled with a ‘Z’. Note that for the Substance Misuse and Maternity Indicators data set, only the first character (A) is submitted.
