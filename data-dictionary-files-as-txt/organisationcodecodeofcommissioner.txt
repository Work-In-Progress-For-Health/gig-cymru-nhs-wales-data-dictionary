(This data item was formerly known as 'Organisation Code (Code of Purchaser)' with the new title and updated definition taking effect from the 21st January 2002. This data item was renamed form ‘Code of Commissioner’ on the 1st October 2009)
 
This is the Organisation code of the health care commissioner. The commissioner code identifies the health care organisation which secures or purchases a patient's treatment during an episode of care.
 
Format:  5 character alpha-numeric
If the commissioner is a Local Health Board/Trust, use the 3 character Local Health Board/ Trust code with two zeros placed in the 4th and 5th character positions.
