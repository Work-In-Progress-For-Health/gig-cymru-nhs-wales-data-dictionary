The number of children under 18 that live in the same household as the client at least one night a week. The client does not necessarily need to have parental responsibility for the children.
 
Format: 2 character alpha-numeric

Meaning

No children live in same household as client at least one night a week.

The number of children under 18 that live in the same
household as the client at least one night a week.
