This indicates whether a person or patient attended for an appointment. If the patient did not attend, it also indicates whether or not advanced warning was given.
Where the response for data item Consultation Method is Virtual Clinical Appointment (Patient Contact) - Video or Virtual Clinical Appointment (Patient Contact) – Phone the recording of data item Attended or Did Not Attend may differ according to the individual scenario.  Please refer to the guidance document for further information.
