The number of days the client has consumed alcohol in the last 28 days (4 weeks) prior to the TOP interview date, as stated by the client.
 
Format: 2 character alpha-numeric.
 
Value
Meaning
00
No alcohol consumed.
01 - 28
Number of days on which the client has consumed alcohol.
99
The client declined to answer.
