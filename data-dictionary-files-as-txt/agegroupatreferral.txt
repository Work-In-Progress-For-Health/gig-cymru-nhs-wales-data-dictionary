Is the age group that applies to the age of the patient at the time the referral is made into secondary care. For open pathways the appropriate age group should be recorded. For all closed pathways the Value 99 Not Known/Not Recorded should be used.
For users calculating the age group based on age in days, use the formula:  Days ÷ 365.25
Format: 2 character alpha numeric

This includes patients on their 16th birthday up to, but not including, their 18th birthday
