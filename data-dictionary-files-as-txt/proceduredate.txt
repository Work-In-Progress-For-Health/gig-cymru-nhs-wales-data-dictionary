This is the date of the start of a patient procedure. Procedure Date Status is associated with this data item and should be used to indicate whether procedure date is supplied or is not applicable.
Format: 8 digit numeric, CCYYMMDD
For Radiotherapy Data Set
Format: CCYY-MM-DD
 
For Postponed Admitted Procedures
This is the date that the planned elective admitted procedure was ultimately carried out. It does NOT refer to the date on which the postponed procedure was due to take place.
