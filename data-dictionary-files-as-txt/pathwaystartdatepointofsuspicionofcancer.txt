The date when a clinician suspects that patient may have cancer and is the start point for the measurement of the 62-day Single Cancer Pathway. In some scenarios, the Date of Receipt of Cancer Referral and the Point of Suspicion of Cancer could be the same day. See Definitions document for pathway start date definitions http://www.walescanet.wales.nhs.uk/scp-key-documents.
 
Format: 8 digit numeric, CCYYMMDD
