The weight of the woman (in kg, to the nearest 100g), as measured at 36-38 weeks, or at onset of labour. The aim is that the information relates to a point as late in the pregnancy as is practically possible.
 
Format: 5 character alphanumeric – nnn.n
 
e.g. 82.5, 102.7
