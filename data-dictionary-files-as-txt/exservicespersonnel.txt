To establish whether the client has advised that they have previously served in the armed forces.
 
‘Previously served’ is defined as anyone who has served for at least one day in HM Armed Forces (Regular or Reserve), or Merchant Seafarers and Fisherman who have served in a vessel at a time when it was operated to facilitate military operations by HM Armed Forces.
 
Format: 1 digit numeric
 
Value
Meaning
1
Client advises that they have previously served in the armed forces
2
Client advised that that they have not previously served in the armed forces
