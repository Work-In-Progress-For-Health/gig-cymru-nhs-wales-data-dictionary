Urgent and Emergency Care Injury Date and Time
Data Set / Collection
Valid From
Valid To
WECDS
1st August 2024
 
 
The date and time that the patient was injured.
 
Where this information cannot be obtained directly from the patient (or patient proxy) the injury date and time should be estimated.
 
Format: 19 character alpha-numeric YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss
 
To avoid confusion, if the injury occurs at midnight the time recorded should never be 00:00, it should always be either 23:59 or 00:01.
