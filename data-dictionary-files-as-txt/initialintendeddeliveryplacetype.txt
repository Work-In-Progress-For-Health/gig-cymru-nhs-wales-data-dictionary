This is the initial intention for place of delivery. The initial intention for place of delivery is that designated by the General Medical Practitioner and hospital staff or by the General Medical Practitioner and the midwife. The decision is normally made when the mother is assessed for delivery, and as a result of this, formal arrangements are made.
Format: 1 digit numeric
