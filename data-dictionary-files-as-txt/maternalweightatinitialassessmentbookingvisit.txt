The weight of the woman (in kg, to the nearest 100g), as measured at the Initial Assessment / Booking Visit, or within the 10-12 week gestation period (when not undertaken at Initial Assessment).
 
Format: 5 character alphanumeric – nnn.n
 
e.g. 82.5, 102.7
