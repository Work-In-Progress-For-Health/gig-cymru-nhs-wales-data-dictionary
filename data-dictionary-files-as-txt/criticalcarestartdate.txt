The date the patient first occupies a designated critical care bed. If there are repeated admissions to the same unit or transfers to different critical care areas within the same Hospital Provider, these should produce separate Critical Care Periods identified by different start dates.
If there are repeated admissions to the same unit during the same calendar day, each period must record and be uniquely identified by the Critical Care Local Identifier.
