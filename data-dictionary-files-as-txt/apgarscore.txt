The apgar score is a measure of the physical condition of a newborn baby. It is obtained by adding points (2, 1, or 0) for heart rate, respiratory effort, muscle tone, response to stimulation and skin coloration; a score of ten represents the best possible condition.
 
FOR NCCHD:
 
This is the total apgar score for a baby at 1 minute and 5 minutes after birth.
 
FOR MI ds:
 
This is the total Apgar score for a baby at 5 minutes after birth.
