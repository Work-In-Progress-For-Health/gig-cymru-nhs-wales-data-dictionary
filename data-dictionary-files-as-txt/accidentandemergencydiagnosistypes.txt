A broad list of diagnosis types, which may be identified during an Accident and Emergency Department Attendance.
 
Notes:
•     At least one Diagnosis Type must be reported
•     Up to 6 diagnosis types can be reported in this section of the Data Set
•     Where a physical injury has taken place; 'Diagnosis Type' and 'Anatomical Area' should have a 1-1 relationship e.g. Abrasion – Hand.
•     For medical conditions where anatomical area is not relevant only a diagnosis type should be recorded.
•     When analysing diagnosis types ALL entries should be considered
 
Format: 3 character alpha numeric
