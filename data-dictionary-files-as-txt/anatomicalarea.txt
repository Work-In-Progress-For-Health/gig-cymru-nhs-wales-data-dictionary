A list of parts of the human body.
Notes:
•     Up to 6 parts of the human body can be reported in this section of the data set.
•     Where a physical injury has taken place, 'Diagnosis Type' and 'Anatomical Area' should have a 1-1 relationship e.g. Abrasion – Hand. .
•     Where multiple body parts have been affected the value '501' should be used.
•     Anatomical area also has a 1-1 relationship with Anatomical side.
Format: 3 digit numeric
