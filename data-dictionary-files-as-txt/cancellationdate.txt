This is the date that the Local Health Board / Trust cancelled the intended event or that the patient notified the Local Health Board / Trust that they did not want the event to occur on the intended date.
 
For the Postponed Admitted Procedure Data Set an event is an elective inpatient or day case admitted procedure.
 
Format: 8 digit numeric, CCYYMMDD
