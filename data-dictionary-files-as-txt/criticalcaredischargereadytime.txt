The time at which the patient has been declared clinically ready for discharge or transfer from the Critical Care Period and a formal request has been made to the hospital bed management system (or appropriate staff with authority to admit at the intended destination) and the date and time of this status is recorded as such in the clinical record.
Format: hh:mm:ss
