The total number of days a patient received level 2 care during a Critical Care Period.
It must be completed when the patient has been discharged from the Critical Care Unit or has died.
There are five recognised levels of adult general critical care in Wales. Descriptions of the type of care captured under each level are set out in the document ‘Designed for Life: Quality Requirements for Adult Critical Care in Wales’: https://dhcw.nhs.wales/information-services/information-standards/data-standards/data-standards-files/quality-requirements-for-critical-care-pdf/
