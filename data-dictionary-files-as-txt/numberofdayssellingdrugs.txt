The number of days over the last 28 days (4 weeks) prior to the TOP interview date that the client has sold drugs, as stated by the client.
 
Format: 2 character alpha-numeric.
 
Value
Meaning
00
The client has not sold any drugs.
01 - 28
Total number of days on which the client has sold drugs.
99
The client declined to answer.
