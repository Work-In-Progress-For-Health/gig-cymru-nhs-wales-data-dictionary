To establish if the client has had an acute housing problem in the last 28 days (4 weeks) prior to the TOP interview.
 
Acute housing problem is defined as:
 
-    The client is of no fixed abode and has been sleeping a night by night basis on the streets.
-    The client has been sleeping in a night shelter on a night by night basis.
-    The client has been sleeping on different friends’ floors each night.
 
Format: 2 character alpha-numeric
 
Value
Meaning
01
Client has urgent housing problem
02
Client does not have urgent housing problem
99
The client declined to answer.
