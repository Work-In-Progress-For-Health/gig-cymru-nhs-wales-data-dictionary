The number of days the client has taken the “other problem substance” in the last 28 days (4 weeks) prior to the TOP interview date, as stated by the client.
 
Format: 2 character alpha-numeric.
 
Value
Meaning
00
No other substance taken.
01 - 28
Number of days on which the other substance was taken.
99
The client declined to answer.
