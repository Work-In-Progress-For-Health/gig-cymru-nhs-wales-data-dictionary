The parity group of the mother. Parity is the number of times a woman has given birth to a live neonate (any gestation) or at 24 weeks or more, regardless of whether the child was viable or non-viable (i.e. stillbirths).
 
Format: 1 digit numeric
 
Value
Meaning
1
Nulliparous – the mother has never previously given birth
2
Primiparous – the mother has previously given birth once only
3
Multiparous – the mother has previously given birth more than once
