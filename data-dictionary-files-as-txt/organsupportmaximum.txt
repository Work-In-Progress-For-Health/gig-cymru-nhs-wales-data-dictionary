The maximum number of organ systems supported on any one day during a Critical Care Period. The duration of each organ system support is calculated using the date from which the organ support was effective and the date the organ support ended. Each organ system can only be counted once on any calendar day. Both basic and advanced categories cannot be counted at the same time. The range of values for Organ Support Maximum is from 0-7.
Format: 2 digit numeric.
