Note: Where a patient has a history of admissions to several hospital providers, then priority between categories 1 and 2 above should be given to the current hospital provider, and category 1 selected, irrespective of whether or not the last admission was to the same hospital provider.
This data item applies only to patients admitted or transferred to a consultant in one of the psychiatric specialties within a hospital provider spell. Record this against the first consultant episode under a consultant in one of the psychiatric specialties but not for subsequent psychiatric consultant episodes or for any non-psychiatric episodes.
