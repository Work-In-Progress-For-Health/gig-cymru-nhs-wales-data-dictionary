This is the date of the booked appointment, treatment or admission.
 
For RTT PTL data set:
For stages 1, 2 & 3 – Where Stage of Pathway = 1, 2 or 3 - ‘Booked Date’ refers to the date on which the patient is due to attend an outpatient appointment or diagnostic procedure.
For stage 4 - Where Stage of Pathway = 4 - ‘Booked Date’ refers to the date on which the patient is due to be admitted as an inpatient or daycase.
In cases where the patient has not been booked for an appointment, treatment, or admission, the field should remain blank.
 
Format: ccyymmdd
