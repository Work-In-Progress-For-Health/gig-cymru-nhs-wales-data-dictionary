This is the priority for the radiotherapy treatment course as classified by the requesting clinician.
Format: 1 character alpha-numeric
 
Value
Meaning
E
Emergency (treatment required within 24hrs)
U
Urgent (to include the Royal College of Radiologist Category I)
R
Routine (to include the Royal College of Radiologist Category II)
D
Elective delay (Treatment delayed for reason)
 
 
For further information on the Royal College of Radiologists Categories see the Royal College of Radiologists website.
