Do NOT put a value in the UCUM Unit of Measurement field for ACVPU* observations.
 
*ACVPU level of consciousness assessment: ‘A’ (Alert), ‘C’ (Confused), ‘V’ (Responds to Voice), ‘P’ (Responds to Painful stimulus) or ‘U’ (Unconscious).
