The number of days over the last 28 days (4 weeks) prior to the TOP interview that the client has undertaken paid work, as stated by the client.
 
Format: 2 character alpha-numeric.
 
Value
Meaning
00
The client has not undertaken any paid work.
01 - 28
Total number of days on which the client has undertaken paid work.
99
The client declined to answer.
