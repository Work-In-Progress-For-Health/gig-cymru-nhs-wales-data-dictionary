Time of discharge from the Hospital Provider Spell. The time on which a patient dies or is discharged from a continuous spell of care using the hospital bed(s) within a single hospital provider. Identical to the end time of the last consultant episode of care and ward stay within a hospital provider spell.
 
Format: hh:mm:ss
