Radiotherapy Diagnosis (ICD) is the International Classification of Diseases (ICD) code for a patient receiving Radiotherapy.  
•         For patients with cancer, Radiotherapy Diagnosis (ICD) is the primary tumour diagnosis code
•         For non-cancer diagnoses, Radiotherapy Diagnosis is the main condition being treated during the Radiotherapy Episode
NOTE: The definition of this data item is different from that of the Primary Diagnosis in this data set.
Format: 6 character alpha-numeric
