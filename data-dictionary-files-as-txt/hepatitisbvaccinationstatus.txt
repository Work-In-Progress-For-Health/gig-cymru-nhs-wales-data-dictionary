To establish whether the client has been offered information AND immunisation against Hepatitis B.
 
Format: 2 character alpha-numeric
 
Value
Meaning
01
The client is already fully vaccinated against Hepatitis B.A client is only considered fully immunized against Hepatitis B when they have received a course of 4 injections
02
Vaccination offered and accepted (this will include clients who are part way through vaccination programme)
03
Vaccination offered and refused.
04
Vaccination not offered.
