The exit status from a treatment modality.
 
Format: 2 character alpha-numeric
 
Value
Meaning
01
Planned exit from treatment modality (i.e. treatment modality completed as set out in the treatment plan)
02
Unplanned exit from treatment modality
 
A planned exit is where treatment has completed, this includes:
 
-    Treatment Complete – Problematic Substance Free
-    Referred to another service
-    Moved to GP Led Prescribing
 
Unplanned exit is where treatment is withdrawn by the provider, this includes clients who:
 
-    Did not attend or respond to follow up contact
-    Moved from area (if client moved from geographical area but was also referred to another service, the latter should be captured.)
-    Retained in custody / prison
-    Deceased
-    Declined treatment
