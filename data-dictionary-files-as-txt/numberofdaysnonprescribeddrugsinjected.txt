The total number of days the client has injected non prescribed drugs in the last 28 days (4 weeks) prior to the TOP interview date, as stated by the client.
 
Format: 2 character alpha-numeric.
 
Value
Meaning
00
No non-prescribed drugs injected.
01 - 28
Number of days on which the client has injected non-prescribed drugs.
99
The client declined to answer.
